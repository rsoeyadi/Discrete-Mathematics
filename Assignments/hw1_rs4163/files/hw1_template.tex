    \section*{Question 8}
    
    If $x$ is even, then $x^2$ is even \\ \\
    \emph{Proof.} $x$ is an even number.\\
    $\exists a$ $\in\Z$ such that $x$ = $2a$ \\
    $x^2$ = $(2a)^2$ = $4a^2$ = $2(2a^2)$ \\
    Let $c = 2a^2, c\in\Z$ \\
    $x^2 = 2c$ \\
    Therefore, $x^2$ is even.\qed
